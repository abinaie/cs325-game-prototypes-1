  • Gameplay: With this game I want the player to test their ability to use their eyes to find objects quickly in a sea of objects. The timer should add a sense of urgency and thrill for the player. The addition of a score will also make the player want to play again and again to see how high of a score they can get. They will also want to play the game again because after playing a couple of times they will know the locations of certain items. Once they find most objects it will become a game of memory to see if they can remember where they saw that particular object during their last game.
